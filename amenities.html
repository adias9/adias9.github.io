Villa Sabine Townhomes is a townhouse community situated in a serene but yet convenient location on Pensacola Beach. The backyard extends to the waterâ€™s edge of Villa Sabine Bay. The Front of the townhome is immediately across the street from the beach. The townhome sleeps up to 10 with 1 king 2 doubles, 4 twin beds with three bedrooms and three full size bathrooms. TV, cable and wireless internet are in every bedroom. A washer and dryer. 3 dedicated parking spaces - 2 car garage parking under the unit with 1 additional space in front of the unit and shared parking on a first come first served basis. Bicycles, paddle boat, 3 kayaks , a volleyball court along with other miscellaneous toys/equipment available for use and stored in garage. A gas grill for cookout . During the summer a trolley service is available free of charge to take you to other parts of the beach. Trolley stop is a few yards down the road and takes you to the center of the beach which is one (1) mile away. It is also a beautiful walk or bike ride on the bike path.
